Spiced Rice

I enjoy listening to Symphonies, Orchestra and Chamber music while cooking as it puts me in the mood of enjoying the experience and ambiance of the kitchen!

Antonio Vivaldi summer from four seasons

Click on the play button to play a sound:

Ingredients:

  • 3 ½ cups of rice
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon cardamom powder
  • ½ teaspoon cinamon powder
  • ½ teaspoon cummin seed powder
  • ½ teaspoon rosemary powder
  • ½ teaspoon star anise powder
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg powder
  • ½ teaspoon clove powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 table spoon of clarified butter
  • 4 cups of water

FYI:Spices and herbs have medicinal benefit to you. Please, consume them frequently in your meals.

Instructions:

  1. Wash rice untill the water is clear.
  2. Combine all the ingridients in a cooking pot.
  3. Measure water in a pot to be one inch above rice surface.
  4. Boil all ingridients for 20 minutes until the water level disapear and the rice is soft and cooked.
  5. Ready to serve with your choice of sauce, toppings or appropiet compliments.
